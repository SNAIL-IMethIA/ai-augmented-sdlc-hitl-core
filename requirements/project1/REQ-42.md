# Strategy leaderboard

**ID:** REQ-42

**Type:** Functional

**Originator:** Stakeholder / Client Interview

**Description:**
The system shall display a leaderboard ranking strategies by their simulation performance metric scores.

**Rationale:**
A leaderboard introduces a gamification element that motivates users to optimise strategies and fosters engagement, particularly in a community or multi-user context.

**Fit Criterion:**

- The leaderboard lists at least the top 10 strategies by a user-selectable performance metric.
- The leaderboard updates automatically after each completed simulation run.
- The ranking metric can be changed interactively, and the list reorders accordingly.

**Customer Satisfaction (0–5):**

- 2: Engagement feature; non-critical to core workflow.

**Customer Dissatisfaction (0–5):**

- 1: Absence has no impact on strategy construction or trading functionality.
