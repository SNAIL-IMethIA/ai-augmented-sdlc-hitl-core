# Visual pipeline graph

**ID:** REQ-17

**Type:** Functional

**Originator:** Stakeholder / Client Interview

**Description:**
The system shall display the strategy pipeline as an interactive visual graph showing all blocks and their directed connections.

**Rationale:**
A visual graph allows users to understand and verify the logical flow of their strategy at a glance, reducing configuration errors.

**Fit Criterion:**

- All blocks and their directed connections are rendered in the graph view.
- The graph updates immediately upon any structural change (block addition, removal, or connection modification).
- The graph accurately reflects the current strategy configuration at all times.

**Customer Satisfaction (0–5):**

- 5: Visual feedback is central to the no-code strategy building experience.

**Customer Dissatisfaction (0–5):**

- 5: Without a graph view, users cannot visually reason about strategy structure.
