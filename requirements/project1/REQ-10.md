# Compare strategies side by side

**ID:** REQ-10

**Type:** Functional

**Originator:** Stakeholder / Client Interview

**Description:**
The system shall allow a user to compare two or more strategies side by side, displaying their configurations and performance metrics simultaneously.

**Rationale:**
Direct comparison eliminates the need for manual note-taking when evaluating variants, and supports data-driven selection of the best strategy.

**Fit Criterion:**

- A comparison view displays the parameters and key metrics of at least two user-selected strategies simultaneously.
- The view clearly highlights differences between the strategies using colour-coding, bold formatting, or another explicit visual indicator.

**Customer Satisfaction (0–5):**

- 2: Significantly accelerates strategy evaluation and selection.

**Customer Dissatisfaction (0–5):**

- 2: Users can compare manually, but this is slow and error-prone.
